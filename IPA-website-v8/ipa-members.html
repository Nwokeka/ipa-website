July 2026 General Meeting — Save the Date

The next General Meeting of IPA UK & Ireland will be held in July 2026. The exact date, time, and venue will be confirmed and circulated to all members via WhatsApp and email at least two weeks in advance. All members are strongly encouraged to attend. 2026 Annual Dues — Payment Reminder

This is a reminder to all members who have not yet settled their 2026 annual dues. The deadline for payment is 31 May 2026. Members with outstanding dues may not be eligible to vote at the next General Meeting. Please make your payment using the bank details in the Dues & Payments section and send your receipt to the Treasurer.

Community Project Update — Igbere Back Home

We are pleased to announce that the first phase of the elderly welfare support project in Igbere has been completed successfully. Funds raised at the October 2025 meeting were disbursed before Christmas. A full project report will be presented at the next General Meeting. Thank you to all members for your generous contributions.
